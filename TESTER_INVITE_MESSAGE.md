Copy and send to your tester list (email, WhatsApp, or Telegram).

**Subject:** You're invited to test BamSignal on Android

Hi,

You're invited to help test **BamSignal** before public launch on Google Play.

**What is BamSignal?**  
A Nigerian dating and connections app built around Signals — not endless swiping.

**What we need from you:**

1. **Join the closed test** using this link:  
   `[PASTE YOUR PLAY CONSOLE CLOSED TESTING OPT-IN LINK HERE]`

2. **Install the app** from the Play Store (it will say “BamSignal” by BamSignal).

3. **Complete signup** with a real email or phone number.

4. **Finish onboarding** — add a photo, pick your state/city, and set preferences.

5. **Send at least one Signal** to someone in Discover (or respond to an incoming Signal).

6. **Test chat** if you get a match.

7. **Try premium/payment** only if you're comfortable — report any checkout issues.

8. **Report bugs** by replying to this message or emailing support via https://bamsignal.com/contact

**Important for Google Play requirements:**  
Please stay opted in to the closed test for **at least 14 days**. We need a minimum of 12 active testers during that period to move toward production.

**What to watch for:**

- App crashes or white screen on open
- Signup/login problems
- Discover not loading profiles
- Signals not sending or not appearing in Likes
- Chat messages not saving
- Photo upload issues
- Voice intro recording (microphone permission)
- Payment/checkout not opening
- Back button closing the app unexpectedly

Thank you for helping us launch BamSignal safely.

— The BamSignal team
