## Screenshots (manual — capture from release APK)

Capture **6–8 phone screenshots** on a real device or emulator (1080×1920 or 1440×2560):

1. Home / city feed
2. Discover profile card (hero photo)
3. Send Signal / match moment
4. Messages / chat
5. Profile with cover photo + verification
6. Premium / Paystack checkout
7. Safety (report/block)
8. Onboarding or WhatsApp verification (optional)

Save as `play-store/assets/screenshots/01-home.png` … `08-verification.png`.

## Upload order (Play Console)

1. Store listing → Graphics → Feature graphic
2. Phone screenshots (minimum 2, recommend 6–8)
3. App content → Privacy policy URL, App access (see `PLAY_REVIEWER_ACCOUNT.md`)
